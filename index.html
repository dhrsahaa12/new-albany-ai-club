ABOUT THE CLUB

Founded in 2025, the New Albany AI Club provides students with opportunities to explore artificial intelligence, robotics, programming, and machine learning through hands-on projects, competitions, and collaborative learning. In our first year, club members earned International Runner-Up honors in the Zero Robotics competition, demonstrating excellence in engineering, programming, teamwork, and problem-solving. Today, members continue to develop technical skills while exploring the future of AI and robotics.

ACHIEVEMENTS

MIT Zero Robotics — International Runner-Up

In its first year, the New Albany AI Club earned International Runner-Up honors in the MIT Zero Robotics competition, a program run in partnership with MIT and NASA where student teams program real satellites aboard the International Space Station. The achievement reflects the team's strength in engineering, programming, strategy, and collaboration on a global stage.
